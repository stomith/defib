About

The Defibrillators bring high-voltage energy to every set. Six musicians who got back on stage because the songs were too good to leave to the radio.

We play town squares, clubs, weddings, corporate parties, and private events across the Vacaville–Davis–Fairfield triangle and the rest of NorCal. Bring your room and your floor; we'll fill them.

From '70s arena rock to '90s alt-radio to today, three hours of songs people know — and want to sing along to.

Honest rock, played by six people who love it.

The Band

Gigi

Lead Vocals

Gigi started her passion for music early, singing along to Streisand, Ann Wilson, Whitney Houston, Pat Benatar, and Linda Ronstadt. She performed on and off stage as a kid, then jumped back on after a 30+ year break — fronting bands again since 2021. "It's never too late to Rock-N-Roll."

Kirk

Lead Guitar

East L.A. by way of Fairfield (nearly 40 years now). The wizard on the 6-string. Plays PRS guitars through Kemper amps.

Ed

Rhythm Guitar

Born and raised in San Francisco's Sunset District; in Vacaville since 2000. Several bands behind him, motorcycles in the garage, retired and making music with friends.

Charles

Bass Guitar

Grew up on the war-torn streets of Stockton honing his chops with the Deftones, Cake, and Jackson Browne — on his turntable. Lives in west Davis, indentured to three cats. Plays 4-, 5-, and 6-string.

Chris

Drums

Originally a guitar player, switched to drums 22 years ago. Santa Cruz raised, Vallejo based, plays with bands across Vacaville. Kits: Gretsch, Yamaha, Pearl.

Glenn

Keyboards

Picked up his first guitar at 60. One jam session with a friend's keyboard later, he was hooked. Lifelong Vacaville resident, retired from a 40-year financial services career. Plays Yamaha CK 88 and Roland Juno DS.
